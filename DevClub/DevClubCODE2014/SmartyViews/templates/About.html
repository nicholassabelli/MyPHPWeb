About Montreal Parking Finder:



Montreal Parking Finder has been coded by 4 graduating computer science students from John Abbott College.

Our goal was to use the parking information found on the City of Montreal website to allow users to find free parking near their current location or their future destination. Our website uses the google maps API and provides coordinates taken from a kmz file. Our current implementation only includes parking spots from Plateau-Mont-Royal, but can and will use more data in the future.

It was designed and implemented within 48 hours as part of the Canadian Open Data Experience project.
